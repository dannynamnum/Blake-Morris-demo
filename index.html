About The Morris Group

A Boca name. A family practice.

Blake Morris is Boca-born and Boca-raised — Saint Andrew's School, then a real estate degree from the University of Miami, and 15 years selling the city's finest homes. His mother, Jeannine, brings decades of Wall Street finance and sales experience. Together, they are The Morris Group at Lang Realty.

The Morris approach is intentionally selective. Rather than carrying twenty or thirty listings at a time, the team takes on a small, curated portfolio — so each client gets full focus, not divided attention. That discipline has produced record-breaking sales, the firm's highest sales recognition, and a long roster of repeat clients.

Born and raised here means knowing more than the market — it means knowing the country clubs, the school districts, the streets, the neighbors. For Boca's most discerning buyers and sellers, that local fluency is the difference.
